# Demo Video Script — The Driving Center SaaS
**Length:** 2 minutes
**Tool:** Loom (or any screen recorder)
**Goal:** Close the sale in 2 minutes

---

## Pre-Recording Checklist

Before you hit record:
- [ ] Login to the app with a test school account
- [ ] Have a CSV file ready with 3 fake students to demonstrate import
- [ ] Have a Loom account open and ready to share
- [ ] Test your audio — make sure you sound confident, not rushed
- [ ] Record in landscape (horizontal), not portrait

## Filming Tips

**What to say if you make a mistake:**
- Don't re-record the whole thing. Just pause for 2 seconds, then say the correct line.
- In post-production, cut the mistake. Viewers don't notice.

**Tone:**
- Confident, not aggressive. You're offering help, not selling hard.
- Speak like you're explaining it to a friend who's a driving instructor.
- Don't rush. Pause between sentences. Silence is fine on video.

**What to have on screen when recording:**
- Login to the app with a real test school (needs credentials first — record this part AFTER setup)
- Alternatively: walk through the Figma wireframes if credentials aren't ready
