## WHAT BUSINESSES PAY FOR (Naval's Truth)

Naval says: "Businesses pay to solve pain, not to gain pleasure."

**Pain categories that = paying customers:**
- Losing money (no-shows, missed payments)
- Wasting time (scheduling, admin work)
- Getting yelled at (customer complaints, DMV issues)

**The Driving Center solves ALL THREE:**

| Pain Point | What Owner Feels | What You Fix |
|---|---|---|
| No-shows | Lost time, lost money | Automated SMS/email reminders |
| Scheduling by text | Hours wasted, errors | Online booking, calendar sync |
| Chasing payments | Awkward, loses customers | Stripe integration, auto-invoicing |
| DMV compliance | Fear of audit | Built-in tracking |
| Instructor management | Chaos with multiple staff | Team dashboard |

**What businesses pay for: Removing their pain.**
