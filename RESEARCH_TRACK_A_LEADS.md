## Next Step

These schools need to be validated by phone before outreach. Use the FSO pattern:
1. Find the owner's name (LinkedIn, Google Business page)
2. Call the school directly — "Hey, I'm building something and I'd like to talk to the owner for 10 minutes"
3. Ask 3 questions: What do you spend the most time on? What breaks most often? What would you pay $99/mo to never deal with again?
